Nejčastější otázky

Otázky, na které se mně klienti ptají nejčastěji. Zakomponovat: odkaz na KDP ČR; jsem pojištěný; portrait
What are the benefits of hiring a tax advisor? A tax advisor can help you maximize deductions, minimize tax liabilities, and ensure compliance with tax laws.
How can a tax advisor help with tax planning? A tax advisor can create a customized tax plan to optimize your financial situation, reduce tax burdens, and plan for future tax obligations.
What documents should I bring when meeting with a tax advisor? You should bring your previous year's tax returns, W-2 forms, 1099 forms, receipts for deductible expenses, and any other relevant financial documents.
How much does it cost to hire a tax advisor? The cost of hiring a tax advisor can vary based on the complexity of your tax situation and the services required. Many tax advisors offer initial consultations to discuss pricing.
Can a tax advisor help with IRS audits? Yes, a tax advisor can represent you during an IRS audit, help gather necessary documentation, and negotiate with the IRS on your behalf.
